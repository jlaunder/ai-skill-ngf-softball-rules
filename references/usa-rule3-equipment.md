# USA Softball 2026 — Rule 3: Equipment

---

## Section 1: The Bat

**A.** The bat must be a softball bat that bears the USA Softball Certification Mark (approval stamp).

**B.** Bats may be made of aluminum, titanium, composite, wood, or any other approved material. **The use of an internal or external bat sensor is allowed provided that the sensor is properly attached.** *(2026 Rule Change 3-1B)*

**C.** Maximum dimensions:
- Length: not more than 34 inches
- Weight: not more than 38 ounces
- Diameter: not more than 2¼ inches at the thickest part

**D.** A legal bat must:
- Have the USA Softball Certification Mark
- Have a grip of cork, tape, or composite material at least 10 inches long
- Not be altered from its original manufactured condition
- Not be damaged or defective

**E.** A damaged or defective bat shall be removed from the game.

**F.** Warm-up bats: Players may use special warm-up bats in the on-deck circle. If a player is using a warm-up bat and accidentally throws it, no penalty is assessed unless it interferes with a play.

**G.** Pine tar/resin: Pine tar or a resin substance is only allowed on the grip of the bat (within the allowable grip area). Applying pine tar above the grip area makes the bat illegal.

**H.** ALTERED BAT: A bat that has been changed from its original manufactured condition is illegal. This includes but is not limited to: rolling, shaving, painting, or applying pine tar beyond the grip area.

**I.** Metal cleats (Spikes): In **Junior Olympic (JO) Girls Fast Pitch** (which includes 12U), metal cleats/spikes are **NOT legal**.

---

## Section 2: Warm-Up Bats

Players may use a special warm-up bat with a padded ring (donut) in the on-deck circle. Use of the donut is optional. Batters may not use multiple bats at once in the on-deck circle.

---

## Section 3: The Ball

**A.** For **12U Junior Olympic Girls Fast Pitch**, the official game ball is a **12-inch softball** meeting USA Softball specifications and bearing the official marking.

**B.** The ball shall be:
- Between 11⅞ and 12⅛ inches in circumference
- Between 6¼ and 7 ounces in weight
- White or yellow (optic yellow is commonly used)
- Covered in leather or synthetic leather
- Stitched with red or white thread

**C.** The home team is responsible for providing game balls.

**D.** Official Softball balls by division (USA Softball):
- 10-inch: 6U, 8U
- 11-inch: 10U (some associations)
- 12-inch: 12U, 14U, Women's Fast Pitch

---

## Section 4: Gloves

**A.** A fielder's glove may be of any weight, size, or shape.

**B.** Gloves may be multicolored. *However:*

**C.** The pitcher's glove/mitt must be one solid color or multi-colored, as long as it does not contain colors that are the same as the color of the ball being used in the game.

**D.** *Note on catcher:* A catcher may use a catcher's mitt (larger glove). No size restriction on catcher's mitt.

---

## Section 5: Catcher's Equipment and Helmets

### A. Batter/Runner Helmets

1. A protective batting helmet with a **double ear flap** must be worn by:
   - All batters
   - All on-deck batters
   - All base runners

2. Helmets must be NOCSAE (National Operating Committee on Standards for Athletic Equipment) approved and must have the USA Softball certification mark.

3. Some associations additionally require a chin strap and face mask — check your league's local rules.

### B. Catcher's Helmet

The catcher must wear:
- A full catcher's helmet (approved skull cap and mask, or full helmet with faceguard)
- A chest protector
- Shin guards (leg guards)
- A throat protector is required to be attached to the face mask or part of the full helmet

### C. Fielder's Masks / Face Masks

- **Required** for all defensive positions in JO Girls play
- Check your league's local rules for any exceptions regarding outfielders.

### D. Face masks for pitchers and infielders

Pitchers and infielders (except 12U/14U outfielders) must wear a face mask while playing defense.

---

## Section 6: Uniforms and Accessories

**A.** All players on a team must wear the same uniform jersey. Numbers must be on the back of the jersey.

**B.** Caps: Players may wear caps, visors, or headbands. For coaches, a cap/visor with team logo is allowed. No mixed headwear on the same team.

**C.** Batting gloves: Players may wear batting gloves.

**D.** Numbers: Numbers must be on the back of the jersey. Illegal for two players to have the same number on the same team.

**E.** Metal cleats/spikes:
- **Not allowed** in JO Girls play (all ages including 12U)
- Metal cleats are legal in Adult Fast Pitch and Adult Slow Pitch, 14 & Under, 16 & Under, and 18 & Under JO Fast Pitch
- **NOT legal in 12U JO Girls Fast Pitch**

**F.** Jewelry: Hard jewelry (rings, bracelets, necklaces) should be removed or taped. Soft, non-abrasive jewelry is permitted. Medical alert bracelets/necklaces are permitted.

**G.** Casts: A hard cast must be padded adequately or the player may not participate.
